Tutorial Lesson 4

Short Story: A Good Meal

The children were hungry. They looked out the windw. Where was their mother? She

walked into the house. The children ran over to her."Mama, we're so hungry ,'they both

said.

She said lunch waas coming. She walked into the kitchen.She opened a can of chicken soup.
She poured the soup into a pot.She added water. She put the pot on the stove. She made
two peanut butter and jelly sandwiches. She sliced an apple. The soup was hot.

She poured it into two bowls. She put the sandwiches on two plates. She put apple slices on
each plate. She put the bowls and plates on the table. The children ran on the table."Thank
you, mommy!"they said. Then they started eating. The cat and the dog watched them eat.
